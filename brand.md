# FinalWhistle brand guide

FinalWhistle is a friendly match-night challenge app for small groups. It should feel energetic and social without borrowing the visual language or promises of a sportsbook.

## Product truth

- The public beta runs only on Solana devnet.
- Supported tokens are real devnet mint accounts with no represented monetary value.
- Copy must never imply cash value, guaranteed availability, a live score when none has been verified, or mainnet readiness.
- Empty, loading, unavailable, rejected, confirmed, and stale states must be named plainly.

## Visual system

- Night: `#062c34`
- Paper: `#fffaf0`
- Green: `#0d795d`
- Lime: `#d7f477`
- Sun: `#ffca5a`
- Coral: `#f1785e`
- Blue: `#5795df`
- Display type: Cooper Black with a sturdy serif fallback.
- Interface type: Avenir/Arial with a system sans-serif fallback.
- Use rounded, tactile cards and a stylized football-pitch motif. Decorative art must not contain invented teams, scores, kickoff times, balances, or transactions.

## Voice

Warm, concise, and honest. Prefer “test tokens”, “challenge”, “pick”, and “result”. Avoid “bet”, “winnings”, “profit”, “live” unless the state is verified, and technical implementation language in primary actions.
